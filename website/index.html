A desktop browser with its own search engine — no tracking, no telemetry, no Big Tech in the middle. Just you and the web.

⬇ Download for Windows Windows 10/11 · ~180 MB macOS & Linux coming soon
🔍

Its own search engine

Bramble runs on an independent web index — not Google, not Bing. Ranked results, image & news search.

🛡️

Private by default

No telemetry, no tracking. Your history and bookmarks stay on your machine. A real private mode when you want it.

🚫

Ads & trackers blocked

EasyList + EasyPrivacy built in, on by default. Faster pages, fewer creeps following you around.

✨

Optional local AI

Turn on AI overviews of your results. The model runs entirely on your machine — nothing is sent anywhere.

First launch on Windows: because Bramble is a new app, Windows may show a “Windows protected your PC” screen. Click More info → Run anyway to continue.
